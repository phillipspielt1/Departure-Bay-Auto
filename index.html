What We Do

Complete Service for Every Vehicle

From routine maintenance to full engine overhauls, we service all makes and models for members of the general public and commercial clients throughout the Greater Nanaimo Area.

Tune-Ups & Diagnostics

Keep your engine running the way it should. We use state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment to find the real issue, not the convenient one.

Brakes

Pads, rotors, calipers, lines, fluid. Brake safety isn't negotiable — we do the job properly every time so you can stop with confidence.

Transmissions

Manual or automatic, we service and rebuild transmissions for imports and domestics. Fluid flushes, clutch replacement, full overhauls.

Engine Overhauls

When your engine needs more than a patch, our mechanics have the experience to rebuild it right. From tune-ups to complete overhauls.

Steering & Suspension

From worn bushings to power steering leaks, we'll get your vehicle tracking straight, riding smooth, and handling the way it used to.

Oil Changes & Maintenance

Regular service is the easiest way to save money on repairs. Oil, filters, fluids, inspections — all to manufacturer spec.
