Our Story

Departure Bay Automotive: Serving Nanaimo 25+ Years

Honest & upfront about our capabilities. At Departure Bay Automotive Service Centre in Nanaimo, we pride ourselves on our ability to offer quality workmanship and good old-fashioned customer service.

Family-owned and -operated, we've developed a positive reputation over the past 25+ years for being honest and upfront about our automotive services and repair capabilities. If it doesn't need a repair, we won't try to upsell it to you. That's because we treat our customers the same way we'd want to be treated.

Whether you drive a daily commuter, a work truck, or the family minivan, you'll find the same careful attention here. We've seen thousands of Nanaimo vehicles come through our shop, and we know how to get yours running at its best.

What We Believe

Straightforward. Reliable. Family.

Our approach is simple, and it hasn't changed in 25 years. These three things guide every job that comes through our shop.

Honest Diagnostics

We tell you what's actually wrong, what can wait, and what you can skip entirely. No invented problems. No scare tactics.

Family Values

We're a family business, and we treat our customers like family. That means patience, clear explanations, and remembering your name.

Quality Work

Every repair is done properly, with the right parts and to the right specs. If it's not good enough for our own vehicles, it's not going on yours.

Education First

Education About Proper Vehicle Maintenance

At Departure Bay Automotive Service Centre, we're also dedicated to educating our customers about proper vehicle maintenance so they can enjoy a longer vehicle lifespan.

If it's time for a tune-up or general diagnostics, we urge you to bring your vehicle in and let us take a look at it for you. Please feel free to contact us with any questions you may have about your vehicle's maintenance. We welcome the opportunity to serve you, our valued customers.

We can also walk you through your maintenance schedule, explain what each service does, and help you plan ahead so you never get surprised by a big bill.
