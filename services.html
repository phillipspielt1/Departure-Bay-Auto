Automotive Services

What We Offer

Complete Services for All Makes & Models

Providing complete automotive services for all makes and models, Departure Bay Automotive Service Centre serves members of the general public and commercial clients alike in the Greater Nanaimo Area.

Our mechanics have years of expertise working on both foreign and domestic vehicles. Whether you need a quick oil change or a full engine rebuild, we'll handle it with the same care and precision.

Please contact us to learn more about our services or to schedule an appointment for a repair or inspection.

Put Our Years of Expertise to Work for You

In addition to keeping your vehicles running smoothly, Departure Bay Automotive Service Centre is also a Designated Inspection Facility licensed by ICBC to provide vehicle inspections.

Whether you're purchasing a used car or are moving from another province, we're capable of providing a thorough mechanical inspection to ensure the overall safety of your vehicle and documentation for insuring it in British Columbia.

Our Full Service List

Everything Your Vehicle Needs

Our automotive services include, but are not limited to, the following. Don't see what you need? Give us a call — we probably do it.

Tune-Ups

Spark plugs, filters, ignition components and engine adjustments to keep your vehicle running smooth and efficient.

Brakes

Pads, rotors, calipers, brake lines, hydraulics and ABS diagnostics for cars, trucks and SUVs.

General Diagnostics

Check-engine lights, unusual sounds, performance issues — we use state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment to find the real cause.

Transmissions

Fluid flushes, service, repair and rebuild work for manual and automatic transmissions on imports and domestics.

Steering

Power steering service, alignment, tie rods, rack and pinion work — anything that affects how your vehicle tracks and turns.

Engine Overhauls

When your engine needs more than a patch, we have the experience to rebuild it properly using quality parts and proper procedures.

Clutches

Clutch replacement, pressure plates, flywheels, throwout bearings and hydraulic systems for manual transmission vehicles.

Regular Service & Maintenance

Scheduled service intervals, fluid checks, filter changes and inspections to prevent problems before they start.

Oil Changes

Quick, proper oil service using the right grade oil and filter for your vehicle. While you're here, we'll do a visual inspection at no charge.

ICBC Safety Inspections

Complete vehicle safety inspections as a Designated Inspection Facility licensed by the Insurance Corporation of British Columbia.

Out-of-Province Inspections

Moving to BC? We provide the complete mechanical inspection and documentation you need to insure your vehicle in British Columbia.

Fleet & Commercial

Commercial clients welcome. We service work trucks, company fleets and commercial vehicles on a schedule that fits your business.

Licensed ICBC Inspection Facility

We're a Designated Inspection Facility licensed by ICBC to offer complete vehicle safety inspections and out-of-province inspections. Fast turnaround, thorough work, proper documentation.
